Call for Lightning Talk Speakers

Finished. Thank you for so many applications.

We are looking for speakers for our lightning talks session during lunch time. Let's liven up lunchtime with some interesting talks!

Details
  • The session will be held in Room A (Kuramae Hall) from 12:30pm to 1:40pm.
  • Lightning talks should last no more than 10 minutes.
  • Lightning talk speakers are required to purchase a ticket for the conference. Currently the tickets have been sold out, but we will arrange extra tickets for the speakers.
  • Unfortunately we are unable to pay travel expenses for speakers.
  • Any talk will be considered, as long as it relates to Scala in some way.
  • Speakers scheduled to present a Sponsor Session may also present separately as an individual.
  • Please refrain from content or jokes that may be deemed racist, sexist or otherwise offensive.
  • Slides will be published on the conference site after the conference is finished.
Schedule

Call for Lightning Talk Speakers deadline: February 15th, 2013. We will contact all applicants by February 22nd, 2013 to inform them of the results.

How to Apply

Please email program@scalaconf.jp, including the following information.

Item Notes
Name No need to supply your real name, workplace, etc., if you don't want to. Just a nickname or Twitter username is fine.
Title A few words to explain the content of your talk. (Max. 15 words)
Description 200 words or so describing the content of your talk in detail.
Self introduction Feel free to include links to blogs, GitHub and Twitter accounts, etc.
