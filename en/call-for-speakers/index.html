Call for Speakers

Finished. Thank you for so many applications.

We are looking for speakers to present at the conference.

Details
  • Any talk will be considered, as long as it relates to Scala in some way.
  • Please refrain from content or jokes that may be deemed racist, sexist or otherwise offensive.
  • Slides will be published on the conference site after the conference is finished.
  • We may record a video of your presentation and publish it on the conference website.
  • We plan to present all speakers with a gift to commemorate the conference. (Stay tuned for more details!)
  • Unfortunately we are unable to pay travel expenses for speakers.
  • Speakers do not need to buy a ticket for the conference. If you have already bought a ticket and you are selected as a speaker, please contact us for a refund.
Schedule

Call for Speakers deadline: July 14th, 2014.(EXTENDED!)

We will contact all applicants by July 30th, 2014 to inform them of the results.

How to Apply

Please email program@scalamatsuri.org, including the following information. The information will be used only as part of the speaker selection process.

Item Notes
Name No need to supply your real name, workplace, etc., if you don't want to. Just a nickname or Twitter username is fine.
Title A few words to explain the content of your talk. (Max. 15 words)
Description 200 words or so describing the content of your talk in detail.
Self introduction Feel free to include links to blogs, GitHub and Twitter accounts, etc.
